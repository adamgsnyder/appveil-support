Need help with AppVeil?

AppVeil helps you audit the permissions and trackers
in the apps on your iPhone, and gives you AI-generated
scripts to negotiate tighter privacy settings.

Common questions:

• How do I scan an app?
Tap the + button on the Apps tab, search the App Store,
and add the app to your audit list.

• What does Negotiate do?
It generates a personalized script and a checklist of
the exact iOS Settings toggles to lock down each permission.

• Does AppVeil collect my data?
No. All audits are stored locally on your device.
See our Privacy Policy for details.
