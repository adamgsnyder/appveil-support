Privacy Policy for AppVeil

Effective date: May 13, 2026

AppVeil is designed to help users review app permissions and trackers on their iPhone and generate privacy-related guidance. This Privacy Policy explains what information AppVeil collects, how it is used, and your choices.

Information We Collect

AppVeil does not require you to create an account.

AppVeil is designed so that audits and related information are stored locally on your device.

We do not intentionally collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location through the app unless you choose to contact support by email.

How Information Is Used

Information stored in the app is used to provide the app’s core features, including app audits, permission reviews, and privacy guidance.

If you contact support, we may use your email and the contents of your message to respond to your request.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

We do not share personal information with third parties for advertising purposes.

Data Retention

Because AppVeil stores audit information locally on your device, you can remove that information by deleting the app or clearing data within the app, if that option is available.

If you email support, we may retain those communications for customer support and recordkeeping purposes.

Children’s Privacy

AppVeil is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information associated with support communications. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Your Choices

You may stop all collection and use of information by uninstalling the app.

You may contact us to ask questions about this Privacy Policy.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will post the updated policy on this page and revise the effective date above.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact: appveilsupport@proton.me
